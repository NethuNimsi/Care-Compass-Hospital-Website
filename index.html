About Us

We're setting Standards in Research
what's more, Clinical Care.

Care Compass Hospitals has been a leader in Sri Lankan healthcare since 1945, known for excellence and innovation. With cutting-edge facilities, and top specialists, we ensure exceptional care. Our advanced units include modern theatres, ICUs, radiology, physiotherapy, and catheterisation labs.

The Heart Command Centre offers world-class cardiac care. With 2,000+ dedicated professionals and JCI accreditation, we uphold the highest global healthcare standards.
